About Us.

Luminous Productions is a small business helping digital creators, influencers, & entrepreneurs by editing their videos professionally. We take our projects very seriously & we make sure to give our clients 120% satisfaction with our service.

Questions & Answers

What software do you use?

The latest version of Final Cut Pro X and adjacent apps in the Apple Suite are used for all editing purposes

What types of videos do you edit?

Our video editing services cover most types of videos ranging from corporate/branded videos to social media, YouTube, explainer videos, real estate, gaming, travel &more!

How soon will I get my content?

Turnaround time for videos is 5-6 days, expedited 24hrs-48hr options are available

Can you match a video editing style?

Yes, we can follow a certain editing style. We can also make sure your videos match your brand style as well.

We just ask that you provide branding/media packages if you have them and references to what you want your project to look like.

How does the whole process work?

The process is super easy!

We get the raw footage sent from you using Google Drive, DropBox, We Transfder or any other similar service.  

We edit the video based on your criteria and get back to with a 1st draft.  

Then we work together with you on finalizing the video and implementing your changes, if any.

What if I can’t afford it?

We understand that our base prices may be high for some creators. That is why we offer free discovery calls for all of our prospects to see if we can deliver a custom package that works with their needs and budget.
